What We Offer

Comprehensive Livestock Services & Products

Our services combine breeding, health, training, agritourism, and farm-based products to improve productivity, profitability, and sustainability for farmers across East Africa.

Livestock Sales

We supply certified Fleckvieh cows together with high-quality breeding inputs and herd support products. All livestock services are quality-controlled and farmer-focused.

Learn More

Artificial Insemination Programs

Our AI services give smallholder and commercial farmers access to world-class semen from proven bulls to upgrade their existing herds without the cost of purchasing a bull.

Learn More

Veterinary Support

Our qualified veterinary team offers vaccination programs and livestock health support to help farmers manage herd wellness and reproduction efficiently.

Agritourism & Farm Visits

We organize educational farm tours for farmers, students, investors, and groups to experience modern livestock farming, breeding technologies, and practical systems.
