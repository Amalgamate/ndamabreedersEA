Welcome to Ndama Breeders EA

East Africa's Most Trusted Livestock Breeding Company

Ndama Breeders East Africa is a premier livestock breeding company dedicated to improving cattle genetics and farm productivity across the region. Founded with a passion for sustainable animal agriculture, we supply carefully selected, health-certified animals to smallholder farmers, commercial ranches, and livestock enterprises across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and beyond.

Our team of experienced veterinarians and breeding specialists works tirelessly to ensure every animal we produce meets the highest standards of health, productivity, and genetic quality.
