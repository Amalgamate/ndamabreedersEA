Our Story

Building East Africa's Strongest Livestock Herds

Ndama Breeders East Africa was founded with a singular vision: to transform livestock productivity across the region by making superior breeding animals accessible to every farmer — from smallholder to large commercial ranch.

With over 15 years of experience in animal husbandry, genetics, and farm management, our team brings deep expertise to every breeding decision. We source and develop only the finest genetic lines, ensuring every animal we supply is capable of delivering outstanding performance in East African conditions.

Our operations span Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and neighbouring countries, with a growing network of farming partners who trust Ndama Breeders to supply animals that deliver real, measurable results on their farms.

About the Founder

Joshua Luvonga Wanjia

The Founder, Joshua Luvonga Wanjia, is an alumnus of Kenyatta University, where he earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Agribusiness and Trade.

He is highly passionate about livestock breeding and empowering small-scale farmers through modern technology to transform the livestock sector by improving efficiency, increasing productivity, and advancing sustainable agricultural development.

Through Ndama Breeders East Africa Ltd, he is committed to providing innovative, practical, and data-driven livestock solutions that empower farmers and contribute meaningfully to food security and economic growth across the region.

Our Farms & Experience

Trusted Since 2011

Ndama Breeders, established since 2011, has been supporting Fleckvieh farmers across the country with good quality Fleckvieh cows.

With our established farms in Kitale at the foot of Mt. Elgon and Kajiado at the foot of Ngong Hills, we have continued to grow and supply the Fleckvieh market through quality breeding, trusted experience, and reliable farmer support.

Our offices include Kitale, with Ngong office coming soon.
